Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian traditional dish and it is very popular

It contains meat pasta and cheese

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Bolognese
  • Cheese
  • Beshamel

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta sheets
  2. Make a Bolognese and cook for 3 hours
  3. Make Beshamel with milk and butter
  4. Add grated cheese
