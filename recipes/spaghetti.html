Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is another italian dish that is popular worldwide

It spaghetti and Bolognese sauce

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Add vegetables and cook for few minutes
  2. Add ground beef and cook until it turns brown
  3. Add tomato sauce, red vine and cook for 3 hours
  4. Cook spaghetti and serve
